Your data, protected

Offline-first, backed up automatically

  • All business data is stored locally on your own computer — the app works fully offline.
  • Scheduled local backups, plus optional backup to your own Google Drive account.
  • Drive access is limited to backup files the app itself creates — nothing else in your Drive is touched.
  • Sign-in tokens are stored in your operating system's secure keychain, never in plain files.

🔐 How Google Drive backup works

When you choose to connect Google Drive, PawnShop ERP asks only for the drive.file permission — the narrowest scope Google offers. It can create and manage its own backup files and nothing more.

You can disconnect at any time from the app's backup settings, or revoke access from your Google Account permissions page. Read the full details in our Privacy Policy.
