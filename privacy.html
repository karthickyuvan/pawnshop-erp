Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026  ·  Applies to the PawnShop ERP desktop application by E3D Designs

1. Who we are

PawnShop ERP is a desktop management application for pawnshop businesses, developed and distributed by E3D Designs ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the application handles, how it is used, and the choices you have.

2. Where your business data lives

PawnShop ERP is an offline-first desktop application. All business records — customers, pledges, payments, cash and inventory — are stored in a local database on your own computer or device. We do not operate servers that receive, store or process your business data, and we cannot see it.

3. Information we collect

  • Licence information. To activate the software, the application verifies your licence key together with a device identifier against our licence service. This is used only to confirm that your copy is genuine and active.
  • Business data you enter. This stays on your device, as described above. It is not transmitted to us.
  • Google account data — only if you choose to connect Google Drive backups, as described in the next section.

4. Google Drive backups and Google user data

The application offers an optional feature that backs up your local database to your own Google Drive account. If you enable it:

  • You sign in with Google using the standard OAuth consent screen.
  • The application requests only the drive.file scope. This permission allows it to create, read and manage only the backup files it creates itself. It cannot see, read or modify any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Backups are uploaded directly from your device to your Google Drive. They do not pass through any server operated by us.
  • Sign-in tokens are stored securely in your operating system's keychain on your device. They are never written to plain files or sent to us.
PawnShop ERP's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Google user data is used only to provide the backup feature you requested — it is never sold, never used for advertising, and never shared with third parties.

You can disconnect Google Drive at any time from the application's backup settings, which removes the stored tokens from your device. You can also revoke the application's access from your Google Account permissions page. Backup files already in your Drive remain under your control and can be deleted by you at any time.

5. How we use information

  • To activate and validate your software licence.
  • To provide the optional Google Drive backup feature, at your request.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.

We do not sell personal information, we do not use it for advertising, and we do not share it with third parties except where required by law.

6. Data security

Your business database remains on hardware you control. Google sign-in tokens are stored in the operating system keychain. Backup files are transmitted to Google Drive over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. We recommend protecting your device with a strong password and keeping regular backups.

7. Data retention and deletion

Because your business data is stored on your own device, deleting it is fully in your control — uninstalling the application or deleting its data folder removes local data. Backup files in your Google Drive can be deleted directly from your Drive. Licence records are retained while your licence is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for support and audit purposes.

8. Children's privacy

PawnShop ERP is business software intended for use by adults. It is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify licence holders through the application or by email.
